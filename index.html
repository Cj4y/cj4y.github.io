Bio

Lals is an artist with inspirations drawing from high fantasy and naturalism as well as dark gritty mystery-thrillers and horror. As a writer, she enjoys writing poetry and bringing about different worlds through comic-book scripts. She claims to have read manga, and other comics for 18 years-and-counting. If she could pick a favourite genre for film or comics, it would be "international" - there's something amazing about being able to hear stories from all around the world. When you don't find her drawing or reading, she is planning the next great adventure - whether that be touring in the mountains locally, or exploring a new culture abroad.
